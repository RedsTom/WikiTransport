## What is a Line?

A line is a route that connects stations in order. Each line has a color, a width, a dash pattern, and belongs to a **transit type** (metro, tram, bus, etc.).

## Creating a Line

1. Open <guide-btn icon="map">Overview</guide-btn> in the left bar
2. In the Lines section, click **+ Add Line**
3. Fill in:
   - **Name** — e.g., "Line A" or "Blue Line"
   - **Transit type** — choose from your existing types (create one first in the Types tab if needed)
   - **Color** — pick a distinctive color
4. Click **Create**

## Adding Stations to a Line

Before a line appears on the canvas, it needs stations. There are several ways:

**Method 1 — Right-click:** Right-click a station and choose <guide-btn icon="add_circle">Add to line</guide-btn>, then pick the line from the submenu.

**Method 2 — Toolbar:** Select the line in the toolbar, then select a station. A <guide-btn icon="add_circle">Add to line</guide-btn> button appears — click it.

**Method 3 — Placement mode:** Select a line, select a station, then click <guide-btn icon="new_label">Add station after</guide-btn> or <guide-btn icon="new_label">Add station before</guide-btn> (icon flipped) in the toolbar. Click on the canvas to place a new station that is automatically added to the line.

### Ordering Stations on a Line

Stations appear on the line in the order they were added. To reorder, open the **Overview** tab and drag stations within a line using the handle icon.

## Selecting a Line

Click the colored dot in the toolbar, or click a line on the canvas. Selected lines appear at full opacity while others dim slightly.

## Line Properties

With a line selected, open the right panel:

- **Name** — rename the line
- **Color** — change the line color
- **Stroke width** — make the line thicker or thinner
- **Dash pattern** — choose solid, dashed, dotted, or dash-dot
- **Transit type** — reassign to a different type

## Removing a Station from a Line

Right-click the station and choose <guide-btn icon="remove_circle">Remove from line</guide-btn>. The station stays on the map but is no longer connected to that line.

## Deleting a Line

Select a line and press `Delete`, or right-click a line and choose <guide-btn icon="delete">Delete line</guide-btn>.
